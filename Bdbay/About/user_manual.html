User Manual

  • Sign-Up Process:

Are You First Visit In bdbayonline ? Want To Create Account ? First You Need To Click Login/Register Button. After Click You Will See A pop-up Box And Here You Can Log-In And On The Bottom "Create New Account" Button. Click It On And It Will Go On Sign-Up Manual And Then You Can Registration.



  • Log-In Process:

Click On To Login Button And Fill The Valid Username And Password. After Fill Field Click On Login Button.



  • Profile Overview:

After Login You Can Access Your Account And There You Can See This Type Of Option.



  • Video Upload:

For Video Upload Click On This Upload Option And It Will Take You Video Post Panel.



  • Video Upload Process:

For Uploading Video Make A Tittle For Your Video. After Fill The Tittle Field, Write Short Description About Your Video. It Will Be Very Helpfull For Video. Now Click On Upload Video And Upload .mp4 File(Note: it will take only .mp4 file make sure that it is .mp4 file). Now You Need To Upload Your Video Thumbnail/Banner, So Click On Upload Thumbnail Option And Select (.jpg, .png, .jpeg) File For Your Video Banner. Now There Have A Posting Type Option. This Field Make Your Video Public/Private. If You Select Public Then All Of The User Can See Your Video Or You Select Private Then Only You Can See Your Video. If You Want To Add Meta Data,Tags,Date,Location,Category etc Then Click On Advance Setting Option And Fill The Field. It Will Be Very Helpfull For Your Video. So Now Click On Publish Button And Wait For Few Minute Untill Publish Your Video.
